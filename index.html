‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I save my game?

A: The game auto-saves every 30 seconds by default. You can also manually save by clicking the "üíæ Save Game" button or pressing Ctrl+S. Adjust auto-save settings in the Settings menu.

Q: What is the Prestige System?

A: Prestige allows you to reset your progress in exchange for Dark Matter, a special currency used for permanent upgrades. You can prestige from the Advanced tab. Higher milestones yield more Dark Matter!

Q: How does offline progress work?

A: When you return after being away, the game calculates up to 4 hours of resource production. Combat and exploration don't progress offline. You can disable this in Settings if preferred.

Q: Why are my resources capped?

A: Resources have storage limits. Build Warehouses to increase capacity (+5,000 per warehouse). Advanced technologies also increase caps.

Q: How do I unlock new ship types?

A: Research technologies to unlock better ships. Each ship requires specific tech: Ion Drive for Corvettes, Plasma Cannons for Destroyers, Shield Tech for Cruisers, and Advanced Weaponry for Battleships.

Q: What are the different enemy types?

A: Enemies range from normal (Space Pirates, Rogue Drones) to elite (Mercenary Squadron, Crystalline Horde) to bosses (Void Leviathan, Ancient Guardian). Each has unique abilities and rewards.

Q: How do specialization paths work?

A: After researching core technologies, you can specialize in Military (combat bonuses), Economy (production bonuses), or Science (research bonuses). Choose based on your playstyle!

Q: What are Mega-Projects?

A: Mega-Projects are expensive, long-term research goals that provide massive benefits: Dyson Sphere (energy), Galaxy Network (all production +200%), and Universal Constructor (costs -50%).

üìã Strategy Tips & Guides

üéØ Early Game Strategy (Pre-Warp Age)

  • Balance Resources - Build 2-3 Metal Mines and Solar Panels each
  • Rush Basic Engineering - Unlocks crucial buildings and ships
  • Build One Warehouse - Early cap increase helps growth
  • Get a Shipyard - Start building a small fleet (5-10 Scouts)
  • Fight Weak Enemies - Space Pirates and Rogue Drones for early resources

üöÄ Mid Game Strategy (Warp Age)

  • Specialize Tech Path - Choose based on playstyle (Economy recommended for beginners)
  • Expand Storage - Build 3-5 Warehouses total
  • Diversify Fleet - Mix of Fighters, Corvettes, and Destroyers
  • Explore Sectors - Claim high-value territories (Ancient Ruins, Trade Hubs)
  • Send Expeditions - Passive resource generation
  • Build Advanced Buildings - Refineries, Banks, Factories

‚öîÔ∏è Combat Tips

  • Check Enemy Power - Only attack if you have 20%+ more power
  • Build Diverse Fleets - Mix ship types for better performance
  • Enable Auto-Combat - Saves clicks for weaker enemies
  • Save for Bosses - Build up fleet before attempting boss fights
  • Upgrade Technologies - Plasma Cannons II & III significantly boost damage

‚≠ê Prestige Strategy

  • First Prestige - Around 500-1000 enemies defeated for ~10 Dark Matter
  • Prioritize Production Boost - Speeds up all future runs
  • Then Research Speed - Faster tech unlocks
  • Cost Reduction - Makes building cheaper
  • Combat Power - For boss-focused playstyle
  • Complete Milestones - Big Dark Matter bonuses

üí° Advanced Tips

  • Resource Management - Use Refineries to convert excess metal to energy
  • Banking Strategy - Banks generate passive credits (useful for trading)
  • Achievement Hunting - Many achievements provide permanent bonuses
  • Challenge Completion - Extra Dark Matter from challenges
  • Keyboard Shortcuts - Use shortcuts for faster gameplay
  • Optimize Production - Match building counts to resource needs
